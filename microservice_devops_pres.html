## Monolithic App Deployments ![img](http://flylib.com/books/3/166/1/html/2/files/fig28_01.jpeg) * Difficult to upgrade live * Especially when dependencies exist between top web stack and lower tiers * Longer time between deployments means higher risk * Likelihood of 'living with' bugs for longer * Benefits of new features take longer to realize
## Monolithic App Deployments ![img](http://flylib.com/books/3/166/1/html/2/files/fig28_01.jpeg) But... * Typically low operational overhead
## Microservices Architecture * Limited functionality in any app means less risk to deploy. * Less risk means more deploys. * More deploys means more features to customers. * More features to customers faster than your competitor is called WINNING!
## Microservices Complexity But, many more apps means we need... * Automated server builds * Configuration management (Puppet, Chef, etc) * Deployment Automation * Self-provisioning Monitors * Load Balancer Automation * Firewall Automation
## This is Devops! In order to harness the competitive advantage of microservices (aka WIN) we must be able to scale fast. This is accomplished by eliminating all manual operations tasks.
